Quick Answers

Your Questions Answered

What sum insured should I choose for my family?
Min. ₹10–15L is recommended for Tier-1 cities. Given 14% inflation, covers today may be insufficient in 5 years. We help you pick plans with high 'No-Claim Bonus' features so your cover grows automatically.
What are waiting periods?
Usually 30 days for new illnesses, and 2–4 years for pre-existing ones. We help you choose policies with the shortest waiting periods and flag specifically what applies to you upfront.
What if my claim gets rejected?
This is where we stand apart. We understand exactly why insurers reject claims. We assist end-to-end in filing grievances with the insurer, Ombudsman, or IRDAI. You're never alone in a claim dispute.
