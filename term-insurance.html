Quick Answers

Your Questions Answered

How much cover do I actually need?
Min. 15–20x of annual income is a base. We help you calculate the exact sum accounting for loans, children's goals, and inflation to ensure a true proxy for your income.
Online vs Advisor — which is better?
Online is slightly cheaper, but an advisor ensures accurate medical disclosure (preventing rejection) and is there during claims. The peace of mind for your family is worth it.
What if my claim gets rejected?
We stand apart here. We understand why rejections happen and how to contest them. We assist end-to-end with the Ombudsman or IRDAI to protect your family's rights.
