Coverage Highlights

What a Good Term Plan Covers

A well-chosen term plan is far more than a basic death benefit. With the right riders, it becomes a complete income-protection shield.

🛡️

Life Cover (Death Benefit)

Lump-sum payout to your nominee on death — ensuring financial stability for your family without disruption.

♿

Accidental Total Disability

If a permanent disability stops you from earning, the plan pays out — protecting your income when you need it most.

🏥

Critical Illness Rider

On diagnosis of cancer, heart attack, stroke, or any of the 64+ critical conditions, receive a lump sum — regardless of survival.

💸

Premium Waiver on Disability

If you're disabled and can't pay premiums, future premiums are waived — your cover continues at no cost to you.

📈

Increasing Cover Option

Cover grows with your life stage — automatically increasing to match inflation, salary growth, and new responsibilities.

👨‍👩‍👧

Income Payout Option

Instead of a lump sum, option to choose monthly income payouts or the combination of lumpsum + monthly income payouts — replicating your monthly salary for years.

🌅

Return of Premium (ROP)

If you outlive the policy term, all premiums paid are returned to you — making the plan cost-free in hindsight.

💊

Terminal Illness Cover

Diagnosed with a terminal illness? Receive a portion of the sum assured immediately — when you need it most, not after.

🔒

Joint Life Cover

Cover both spouses under a single plan — ensuring both incomes are protected, especially valuable for dual-income families.

Choose Wisely

Term Plan vs ULIP / TULIP vs Traditional Endowment

Many families are sold expensive investment-linked plans when a pure term plan is all they need. Here's an honest comparison.

Feature ⭐ Pure Term Plan ULIP / TULIP Endowment / Money-Back
Primary Purpose Pure life protection Insurance + investment Insurance + savings
Sum Assured for ₹1K/mo premium ₹1 Crore+ ₹15–30 Lacs ₹1.35 Lacs
Cost Per Lac Lowest High Highest
Maturity Benefit ROP available ✓ Market-linked ✓ Guaranteed
Flexibility of Riders ✓ Highly flexible Limited Very limited
Best For Families needing max protection Investors with risk appetite Conservative savers
Recommended Cover Min. 20x of annual income Supplement, not primary Not ideal as primary cover
💡 Our honest advice: Buy a pure term plan for maximum protection at minimum cost, and invest the premium savings separately in mutual funds. This "buy term + invest the rest" strategy almost always beats ULIPs and endowments over a 15–20 year horizon.
