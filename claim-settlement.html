Your Questions Answered

Rejected months ago. Still have a chance?
Yes. Typically you have 1 year for Ombudsman and 2 years for Consumer Court. Share your rejection letter; we'll check the window instantly.

Absolutely. Partial settlements are very successfully challenged. If sub-limits or deductions were unfair, we file for the difference.

That's mis-selling. It's an IRDAI violation. We help you gather evidence and file for recovery of dues through Consumer Forum.
