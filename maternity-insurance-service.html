Questions Answered

Quick Answers

Can I buy this if I'm already pregnant?
Yes, you can buy the policy. However, this specific delivery will not be covered due to the 12-month waiting period. Your next delivery will be fully covered as long as the policy remains active.
Can I continue the policy after my deliveries are done?
Absolutely. This policy comes with lifetime renewability. After your maternity needs are fulfilled, it continues to serve as a comprehensive health insurance plan for your whole family.
Can I buy this policy on EMI?
Yes, multiple EMI options are available: 1) Directly through the insurance company, 2) Through your credit/debit cards, or 3) Through 'Loan Against Mutual Funds' (which is often the most cost-effective way).
